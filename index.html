Why do cats like boxes?

Cats love boxes because they are cryptic animals; they like to hide from predators and they like to stalk prey. Boxes provide a place for cats to hide, stalk, and pounce on their prey. Boxes also provide cats with a cozy, safe place to sleep, which is very important for cats.

Why are cats adorable?

Cats are adorable because they are cute, cuddly, and playful. Cats are also adorable because they are soft, fluffy, and furry. Cats are also adorable because they are funny, silly, and goofy. Cats are also adorable because they are curious, intelligent, and independent.
